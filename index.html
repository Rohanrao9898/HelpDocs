Setting Up Email in the Gmail App

If you're looking to configure your email account in the Gmail app, follow these step-by-step instructions:

1. Open Gmail App

Open the Gmail app on your device.

2. Tap "Other" while logging in

Select the "Other" option to set up your email.

3. Enter Email and Password

Enter your email address and password, then tap "Sign in."

4. Tap "IMAP account"

Tap "IMAP account" to proceed with the setup.

5. Verify Account Information

Under "Account," ensure that your email address, username, and password are filled in.

6. Verify Incoming Server Settings

Under "Incoming server," verify the following settings:

  • IMAP server: imap.secureserver.net
  • Security type: SSL
  • Port: 993
  • IMAP path prefix: Leave this blank

7. Verify Outgoing Server Settings

Under "Outgoing server," ensure the following settings:

  • SMTP server: smtpout.secureserver.net
  • Security type: SSL
  • Port: 465
  • Require authentication to send emails: On

8. Tap "Next"

Tap "Next" to proceed with the setup.

9. Set Email Sync Preferences

Choose your "Email sync period" and "Email sync schedule" options, then tap "Done."

10. Update Account Name and Your Name

Update your "Account name" and "Your name" (the name that will appear on sent emails), then tap "Done."

Please note that specific steps and menu names might vary slightly based on the version of the Gmail app and the device you are using. Refer to the Gmail app's documentation or support resources for the most accurate and up-to-date information.
